Brilliance delivers orders to regional areas using PAXI courier service. You'll receive updates on your delivery directly from them once your order is in their hands.

Delivery will be made within 3 to 5 working days from the date your order was placed, between 9am and 5pm (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays). Delivery to regional areas can take up to 7 working days. Brilliance supports delivery within South Africa.

Free Delivery

Delivery is free for orders valued at R500 or more. A delivery fee of R70 applies for orders under R500. The same delivery timeframes apply.
